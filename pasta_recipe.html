How to Cook Pasta

  1. Heat a pan of water
  2. Add some salt
  3. Add a package of pasta
  4. Cook 25 minutes
  5. Add two cup of cold water then to strain a pan of pasta
How to Cook Sos of Pasta
  1. Add some oil in the pan
  2. Add two spoon of tomato paste and to roast 2 minutes
  3. Add some spices, you should use chili pepper , salt ,mint black peper
  4. Add sos in the a pan of pasta and to mix them
